CAPM
Capital Asset Pricing Model. Expected return: E[R] = Rf + β·(E[R_m] - Rf). We convert this annual expectation to a monthly log mean for compounding.
β (beta)
Sensitivity to the market: β = cov(R_i, R_m) / var(R_m). β > 1 ⇒ moves more than the market; β < 1 ⇒ less.
Risk-free rate (Rf)
Baseline return with (assumed) no risk, e.g., short-term T-bills.
Equity Risk Premium (ERP)
The market’s expected return above the risk-free rate: E[R_m] - Rf.
